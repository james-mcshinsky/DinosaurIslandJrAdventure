Dinosaur Island Jr. Adventure

1–5 junior park managers draw DNA, hatch dinos and welcome visitors to their tropical island dinosaur park.

Rule Book Play Online

Game Overview

Manage your Lab to extract DNA and discover new dinosaurs. Shop in the Market for attractions, specialists and upgrades. Build your park and welcome visitors to earn coins and victory points!

  • 1–5 Players
  • Age 6+
  • 30–60 Minute Play Time

How to Win

Earn victory points by exhibiting dinosaurs, building attractions, completing objectives and converting leftover resources at the end of the game.

Round Structure

  1. The Lab — gather DNA and discover dinosaurs.
  2. The Market — buy attractions, upgrades or specialists.
  3. Upgrade Your Park — hatch dinos and improve security.
  4. The Park Opens — draw visitors for money or VP.
  5. Cleanup — refresh components and advance the round.
